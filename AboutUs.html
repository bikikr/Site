Who Are We?

Ok, so we get asked a lot, why Cloud Wing? Why Wing? Why not some acronym or initial grouping or catching title that includes the latest buzz phrase? The answer is simple because that’s not who we are. We wanted a moniker that captured what we do, who we are. We knew we were a group of experienced professionals--who had successfully launched and grown brands for our clients, collectively.

So, after thoughtful contemplation, we chose Cloud Wing, because it means a type of fin that produces LIFT. Because really that’s what we do—Cloud Wing Technologies gives life to powerful new brands and a new lift to static ones.

We also loved that in mathematics, especially in the area of geometry known as Wing theory, which is a mathematical model that predicts lift distribution over a three-dimensional wing based on its geometry. That three-dimensional concept fits our philosophy and strategy for a successful branding campaign— Study/Research, Analysis, Creativity, Action/Implementation and Results/Brand Growth. And then the cycles begin again.
